								<h1>
	Environmental Studies/Biology Combined Major B.A.
</h1><div id="degree-req">
	<div id="degree-req-1">
		<h2>
			Information and Policies
		</h2><h3 class="sc-RequiredCoursesHeading1">
			Introduction
		</h3><p>This course of study provides students with the basic tools of biological science and sufficient understanding of resource conservation, conservation biology, and concerns about environmental sustainability to apply these tools to environmental problems.</p>

<p>Students interested in the available concentrations should choose to pursue the environmental studies B.A. degree. This does not apply to students who declared a combined major and concentration in agroecology and sustainable food systems prior to 2019.</p>
<table>

		</table><h3 class="sc-RequiredCoursesHeading1">
			Academic Advising for the Program
		</h3><p>Advising is one way to make the most of your university experience. The advising system at UC Santa Cruz is amazing, and we encourage you to use it often. Ask questions, seek advice, and make decisions that work best for you.</p>

<p>To receive advising for this major, contact <a href="mailto:envsadvi@ucsc.edu">envsadvi@ucsc.edu</a>. Additional information for prospective transfer students can be found in the Transfer Information and Policy section.</p>
<table>

		</table><h3 class="sc-RequiredCoursesHeading1">
			Program Learning Outcomes
		</h3><p>Students graduating with a B.A. degree in environmental studies/biology combined&nbsp;will be able to:</p>

<ul>
	<li>
	<p>Identify the societal (social, political, economic and ethical) agents and structures that contribute to environmental change. (social science competency)</p>
	</li>
	<li>
	<p>Describe the structure and functioning of major physical and ecological components of the earth&rsquo;s systems. (natural science competency)</p>
	</li>
	<li>
	<p>Access and analyze a complex literature addressing specific topics in environmental studies, and evaluate the usefulness and limitations of individual sources of information. (analytic thinking)</p>
	</li>
	<li>
	<p>Demonstrate effective oral and written communication skills. (communication skills).</p>
	</li>
</ul>

    </li>
